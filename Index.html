What are the key differences between sparse and dense retrieval in RAG systems? Can you cite sources from my documents?
🧬
Based on your research documents, here's a comparison of sparse vs dense retrieval:

Sparse Retrieval (BM25/TF-IDF)
• Keyword-based matching using exact term frequency
• High precision for exact terminology queries
• Computationally lightweight, no embeddings needed
• Poor semantic understanding

Dense Retrieval (Embeddings)
• Semantic similarity via vector representations
• Handles synonyms and paraphrases effectively
• Requires embedding model (this system uses text-embedding-3-large, 3072-dim)
• Higher recall for conceptual queries

Hybrid approaches combining both (RRF fusion) typically outperform either alone by ~8-15% on benchmark datasets. [Confidence: High]
